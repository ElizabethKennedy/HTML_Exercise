Elizabeth enjoys literature, astrology, yoga, and roller skating. She is a Pisces sun sign, Libra moon sign, and Aries rising sign. She extracts highly practical everyday applications from the esoteric arts. Coding intimidates her, but she is determined to succeed at it, anyhow. Her level of stubbornness is the stuff legends are made of -- her old high school principal once called her "relentless" -- so she isn't fazed by having "total newbie" status in the Information Technology world. Elizabeth has a ten-year old daughter who is her version 2.0 clone and regularly provides her with sassy, exasperated tech support.
